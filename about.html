About Mr. Bob Li

Mr. Bob Li graduated from a rigourous 5 year undergraduate program in Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) from the Shang Dong University of Traditional Chinese Medicine in China. After graduation, Mr. Li practiced as a TCM doctor in two large TCM hospitals in China.

From 2001 to 2004, Mr. Li was a master degree candidate for a 3 year program in traditional Chinese medicine (TCM) from Guangzhou University of Chinese Medicine.

For over 20 years, Mr. Li has accumulated invaluable and extensive various clinic experience in treating and healing various health issues by using combination of acupuncture, cupping or Tuina massage techniques or natural herbs. In 2012, Bob Li and his family immigrated to Ottawa, Canada.
